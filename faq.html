FAQ

Yield farming on this dashboard refers to the method of earning rewards by providing liquidity to DeFi protocols through the available features.

To stake your tokens, navigate to the Staking section on the dashboard, select an available staking pool, and follow the instructions to complete the transaction.

Potential risks include impermanent loss, market volatility, and smart contract vulnerabilities. It is important to understand these risks before participating.

You can monitor yield farming rewards and staked tokens by visiting the Portfolio section on the dashboard. There you will find detailed statistical reports and earnings.

You can learn more about yield farming and staking strategies by visiting our Resource Center and community forums for expert advice and the latest updates.
